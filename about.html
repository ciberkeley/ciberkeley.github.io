About Us

History

Founded in 2012 and reincorporated in 2014, Capital Investments at Berkeley was created by and for students striving to obtain real trading experience and provide investors superior risk-adjusted returns. In a perpetually changing market environment, CIB prides itself in our creative approach to trading and research, implementing a wide variety of data using a large selection of financial instruments.

Culture

Our members are our greatest asset. They are drawn from a multitude of academic and cultural backgrounds. This gives CIB diversified perspectives and insights on global markets, also providing the group with a worldwide alumni resource. CIB fosters a fast paced learning environment, where collaboration is key. That connection extends far beyond the classroom, cultivating life-long friendships.

Philosophy

Every decision answers a question. At CIB we focus on the questions we ask just as much as the decisions we make. At the crux of our philosophy is a multimensional approach to problem solving. When we evaluate risk, we think about more than volatility. We evaluate investment externalities, time value, and emotional bias. This allows us to make sound investment decisions, leading to truly risk-adjusted returns. Our appraoch to returns is also multidemensional. Our returns strech far beyond the reach of our portfolio, encapsulating the success of our members, unity of our network, and longevity of Capital Investments at Berkeley.

Alumni & Network

Capital Investments at Berkeley benefits from a tight-knit network of alumni, holding experience in a variety of industries.
